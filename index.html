1. Health and Biomedical Technologies

The core of our work lies in Health-Tech, where we develop AI and data-driven models using wearable sensors for the objective assessment and monitoring of individuals. Our systems enable early diagnosis, tracking, and rehabilitation of neurological disorders such as Parkinsonâ€™s disease and post-stroke recovery. We also advance intelligent methods for elderly care and cardiovascular monitoring, integrating multi-sensor data fusion and predictive analytics to enhance precision medicine and personalized healthcare.

2. Environmental and Disaster Technology

Our lab applies AI, remote sensing, and Earth observation technologies to address pressing global challenges. Research includes cloudburst and flood prediction through spatio-temporal modeling, deforestation detection using deep learning on satellite imagery, and climate resilience analysis leveraging geospatial and LLM-based reasoning systems. This interdisciplinary effort bridges data science and environmental sustainability through intelligent monitoring and early warning solutions.

By maintaining a core focus on intelligent systems while integrating these diverse application areas, our research strives to translate cutting-edge computational methods into high-impact, real-world solutions that enhance health and environmental safety.
